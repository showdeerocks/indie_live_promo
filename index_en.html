About the Festival

On Saturday, August 2nd, 2025,
the day Pentaport Rock Festival takes place,
for musicians who weren't invited to Penta
and audiences who can't go to Penta,
the underground rock festival, Panty Port opens its doors.

No rehearsal or soundcheck, 5 hours of continuous music
with 10 artists performing 30 minutes each.
